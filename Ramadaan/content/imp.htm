What is Islam Media Player ? Islam Media Player is a revamped, much more advanced Software compared to the successful Quraan Player, Because of the success of Quraan Player and the need and the request for more recitors , Islam Media Player was born. Islam Media Player now allows us that flexibility to cater for different recitators across multiple cds.

How does Islam Media Player work ? Islam Media Player is a piece of software that can control Quraanic recitations from various recitors across multiple of CDS. These CDs are categorized into Volumes. What this means is that you will have one software on your hard drive, and you can play any volume you like by simply selecting the options in Islam Media Player.

These new volumes allow you to customise Islam Media Player according to your needs and lets YOU choose your favourite recitors.
